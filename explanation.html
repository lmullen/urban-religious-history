To use this map, select a city and a range of years, or click a city marker on the map. Clicking the map backround will display all cities.

This is an interactive bibliography of books published about religious history in a particular American city. Its aim is to show where historians of American religion have focused their attention—and where they have not. You can read Paul's account of why we made this site and Lincoln's explanation of what the map shows at the Religion in American History blog.

Have we left out a book? Please e-mail Paul <paul.putz@gmail.com> and we will update our data.
